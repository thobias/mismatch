Test trials

We will start with three test trials for you to get comfortable with the interface. Two images of faces will be presented at the top left and top right of the window half a second after you click the start button. You get 2 seconds to look at the pictures, do not move the mouse during this period. After this, click on the face which you found more attractive.

You will get a follow-up question regarding how sure you are of your choice. After this the chosen image will be shown again and you are asked to select the main reason for choosing this face. This completes one trial.
